Description of the service

TableautoDbt provides browser-based tools for working with Tableau workbook files (.twb and .twbx), including:

  • Convert: Translates Tableau calculated fields into dbt SQL models for Snowflake and BigQuery
  • Diff: Compares two Tableau workbook versions and surfaces changes across calculated fields, parameters, data sources, sheets, and filters
  • Docs: Extracts and browses the contents of a Tableau workbook including formulas, parameters, data sources, and filters
  • Audit: Analyses a Tableau workbook for quality issues, anti-patterns, and formula problems

Most processing occurs entirely in your browser. The Convert tool includes an optional AI translation feature that sends calculated field formulas (not metadata) to a third-party API.
