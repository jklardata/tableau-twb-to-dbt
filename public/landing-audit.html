What You Get
A health check for your Tableau logic.
Instantly surface hidden problems in your workbook before they become production issues. Complexity scores, unused fields, circular deps, and more.
❤️
Health Score
A 0-100 score weighted by errors, warnings, and info-level issues across every calculated field.
🔴
Error Detection
Flags circular dependencies, division without null protection, and deeply nested LOD expressions.
🟡
Warning Detection
Catches unused calculated fields, excessive nesting, long IF chains, and non-additive window functions.

Complexity Scoring
Every calc field scored 0-100 across four tiers: simple, moderate, complex, and critical.
🔗
Circular Dependencies
Detects fields that reference each other in a loop, which can cause silent errors in Tableau.
🗑
Unused Field Detection
Identifies calculated fields that exist in the workbook but are not used in any sheet or filter.
